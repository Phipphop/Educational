☀ Solar Grid

You run a renewable mining outpost. The sun powers everything — but nights grow longer, and the richest ore (Iridium) can only be mined in the dark, off stored battery power.

  • Generate: place Solar Panels (daytime only).
  • Store: Batteries bank surplus sun for the night.
  • Distribute: Pylons carry power — buildings must sit inside a pylon's range. Each hop from the Hub loses a little energy.
  • Mine: Extractors sit on ore nodes and run only while powered. Iridium needs lots of power, at night.
  • Goal: deliver 30 Iridium. Lose if your Hub goes dark for 60s.
