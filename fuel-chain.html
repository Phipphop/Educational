🛢 Fuel Chain

Gas doesn't appear at the pump. Before a car can burn it, someone has to pump crude oil, refine it, ship it across the sea, and truck it to a station — and every one of those steps burns energy.

Build that chain yourself. Your job: keep the gas station across the water supplied until you've delivered the goal amount of fuel to cars.

  • Pump: place a Pumpjack on the oil field (left). It runs on electricity.
  • Connect: lay Pipe from the well to a Refinery, then to an Export Port on the coast.
  • Ship: build an Import Port on the island. Tankers cross automatically, trucks deliver to the station.
  • Watch the meters: every step adds to the electricity and fuel the chain quietly consumes.
